üìñ How to Use

Adding New Prompts

1

Open Prompt Manager

Click the extension icon and select "Manage Prompts" to open the settings page.

2

Enter Shortcut & Prompt

Type a memorable shortcut (e.g., "summarize") and your full prompt text.

3

Save Your Prompt

Click "Add Prompt" to save. Your prompt is now available across all platforms!

Using Prompts

1

Type "//" in the Input Box

In any supported AI platform, click the message input and type "//".

2

Filter Your Prompts

Continue typing to filter (e.g., "//rev" shows prompts starting with "rev").

3

Select & Insert

Use arrow keys to navigate, then press Enter or Tab to insert the prompt.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

The autocomplete isn't showing up. What should I do?

Make sure you're on a supported platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DuckDuckGo, or Microsoft Copilot). Try refreshing the page. Check that the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions/.

My prompts aren't saving. How can I fix this?

Click the "Refresh" button in the popup. Check Chrome's storage permissions. Try removing and re-adding the prompt.

Can I use this extension on multiple computers?

Yes! Use the Google Drive sync feature to keep your prompts synchronized across all devices where you have the extension installed.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All prompts are stored locally on your device. If you use Google Drive sync, your data is stored in your private Google Drive account. We don't collect or have access to any of your data.

How do I share prompts with my team?

Use the "Export JSON" feature to download your prompts as a file. Share that file with your team members, who can then use "Import JSON" to add those prompts to their collection.

Does this work with other browsers?

Currently, AI Prompt Assistant is available for Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, etc.).
